About Me

Hi, I'm Sohan PM. I'm currently working as a research scholar in physical oceanography at the CSIR-National Institute of Oceanography, Goa. My research focuses on understanding the physical processes of the ocean and their impact on the environment. I have a keen interest in data analysis, utilizing various tools and techniques to derive meaningful insights from oceanographic data.

Portfolio

Here are some of the projects I have worked on:

  • Project 1: Analysis of ocean current patterns in the Arabian Sea using Python and xarray.
  • Project 2: Simulation of storm surge events using the WRF model.
  • Project 3: Visualization of wave height data from the WW3 model.
  • Project 4: Development of an IDL script for processing satellite imagery of sea surface temperature.
